English Translation

Arjuna said: O Supreme Person, what is Brahman? What is the self? What is karma, O Purushottama? What is said to be the material manifestation, and what is called the demigods?
Who is the Lord of sacrifice and how does He dwell in the body, O Madhusudana? And how can those engaged in devotional service know You at the time of death?

English Translation

The Blessed Lord said: The indestructible, transcendental living entity is called Brahman, and his eternal nature is called the self. Action pertaining to the material bodies of living beings is called karma.
The physical manifestation is called the perishable, and the universal form (Virat Purusha) is called the demigod. I am the Lord of sacrifice, residing in this body, O best of embodied beings.
