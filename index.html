Intro

Introduction to the Bhagavad Geeta

What is the Bhagavad Geeta?

The Bhagavad Geeta is a 700-verse Sanskrit scripture that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata. It is a dialogue between Lord Krishna and Arjuna, set on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

Author and Origin

It was narrated by Lord Krishna and written down by Sage Vyasa. It provides philosophical and practical guidance for daily living.

Why is it Important?

Geeta teaches us how to live with purpose, stay calm in challenges, and connect with our true self. It is relevant even today — for students, leaders, and seekers of peace.

About

Why This Website?

This website is created to share the divine knowledge of the Shreemad Bhagavad Geeta with people across the world. It is designed for readers of all ages who wish to learn and reflect on the timeless wisdom spoken by Lord Krishna.

Our Vision

We believe that the teachings of the Geeta can help people find peace, clarity, and purpose in today’s challenging world. Our aim is to make the Geeta accessible in multiple languages—Sanskrit, Marathi, Hindi, and English.

About the Creator

My name is Pooja Deshmukh. I’m passionate about spirituality, learning, and sharing knowledge. This website is a humble offering towards spreading the eternal message of the Bhagavad Geeta.
